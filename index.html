Our story

It was 2019 that I and my friends-Bami,Preye and Anjola started thinking of serving sweet, black cups of coffee to my wonderful customers in FUTA. This coffee, a mythical bridge between the blended chocolate drink and a strong black cup, needed to combine the delicate flavors only present when the coffee is grown and processed with love and care. Out of this thought John's Coffee Shop was born in January 2020. We sell coffees in small releases with the kind of customer service that meets people where they are - a solid, kind approach built on a desire to please them not only in exchange for money, but with the drink they sip over meetings, laptops and books.

What we do

We are devoted to delivering the finest and best brewed coffee to coffee-lovers. We source only the highest quality coffee beans just to give our customers that Bold and rich flavor taste in every cup of coffee. Our coffe is freshly made upon your order. It is packed in a clean disposable cup with a nice cover. We deliver in sections Breakfast, Brunch-mid-day also in special cases early evenings. We have services for bulk services and discounts also. We also have special discount packages for couples. There are also varieties of flavors prepared for your consumption.

How it works

Our coffee at John's Coffee shop is hand made and mixed with the best brewed coffee beans. When you choose and purchase from our quality products, of various flavors. Your coffee is made out of Love and delivered to your location in 30-60 minutes depending on your Location.
